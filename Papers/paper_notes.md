### Pretraining dynamics:

##### Probing:
[Probing Across Time: What Does RoBERTa Know and When?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.07885): This paper focuses on probing a RoBERTa language model through pretraining checkpoints, examining its linguistic, commonsense, factual, and reasoning capabilities.

[Pretrained Language Model Embryology: The Birth of ALBERT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.02480): Examining linguistic and some factual capabilites through pretraining checkpoints of an Albert model. Also evaluated on some downstream tasks over pretraining steps.


##### Training loss and models' influence from training data:
[How Do Large Language Models Acquire Factual Knowledge During Pretraining?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.11813): By crafting a fictional knowledge dataset they demonstrate how would models react in terms of training loss metrics to new knowledge. Also using paraphrased, duplicates, and compositional examples they probe the level of the knowledge acquisition. Also they analyzed how the frequency of exposing new knowledge during pretraining would affect its learning.


### Knowledge in LLMs:
[Knowledge Mechanisms in Large Language Models: A Survey and Perspective](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.15017): A survey paper that reviews the mechanism across the whole knowledge life cycle in LLMs.
